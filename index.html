About us

Justwood is one of the advocates of environmental conservation, in which products are made up of reclaimed hardwood from old houses in the Philippines. These woods are renewed and perfected to bring comfort and satisfaction. Each piece of classic vintage furniture entails a memoir which will be part of your own.
